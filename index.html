Swedish massage

This massage technique involves deeper and faster massage movements to assist with sore muscles, tension and increased circulation. The classical massage movements and their benefits over the full body as well as the back, neck and shoulder area.

Facial

Waxing

Waxing is the process of hair removal from the root by using a covering of a sticky substance, such as wax, to adhere to body hair, and then removing this covering and pulling out the hair from the follicle.

Make-Up

Manicure & Pedicure

Traditional manicure and pedicure treatments performed on the natural nail on both the hands and the feet. It involves filling, shaping and trimming of the cuticles, removing hard skin on the feet, massage and finishing the nails with a nail paint.

Aromatherapy

Aromatherapy is a holistic healing treatment that uses natural plant extracts to promote health and well-being. Often known as essential oil therapy.
